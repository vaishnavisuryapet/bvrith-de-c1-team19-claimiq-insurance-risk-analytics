# Final Demo Script

**Week:** 12  
**Time target:** [10–15 minutes depending on mentor instructions]

---

## 1. Opening — 1 minute

- Introduce team and project.
- State the engineering problem.
- State final outcome.

---

## 2. Architecture Walkthrough — 2 minutes

Explain:

Raw Sources → Bronze → Silver → Data Quality → Gold → Power BI → Streaming Simulation

---

## 3. Dataset and Source Design — 2 minutes

- Show source files.
- Explain keys, assumptions and controlled data quality issues.

---

## 4. Databricks Pipeline — 4 minutes

- Show Bronze ingestion.
- Show Silver standardization.
- Show DQ checks.
- Show Gold metrics.

---

## 5. Power BI Dashboard — 2 minutes

- Open dashboard.
- Explain KPI cards, trends, filters and insights.

---

## 6. Streaming Simulation — 2 minutes

- Show JSON event files.
- Explain Auto Loader / Structured Streaming output.
- Show live/near-real-time metric.

---

## 7. Closing — 1 minute

- Summarize what the team learned.
- Mention limitations and future improvements.
- Thank mentors and reviewers.
